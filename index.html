The Architecture
of Trust

Every product in the portfolio shares the same foundational design decisions. Not by accident — by principle.

∅
Zero Data Held
No databases of user information. Plans, cases, and audit reports live in the user's own Google Drive. We build the tools. You own the data. No breach risk. No retention liability. Always.
◈
Verified by Design
AegisAudit generates cryptographic ZK proof audit trails. Furestate notarizes on Polygon. Swift Verdict verdicts are immutable and binding. Trust isn't claimed — it's provable.
§

The SSAF detection method is patented (US 63/835,655). The quantum-resistant fraud prevention application is separately patented. The moat is scientific, legal, and structural.
⊙
Open Research Foundation

⇄
Self-Reinforcing Portfolio

"I didn't set out to discover a new attack surface. I set out to run an experiment, and the cloud models 404'd."

Dustin Tyler James is a self-taught inventor and independent AI researcher operating under Pay Dirt Global LLC. In 2024, during a routine experiment on multi-model orchestration, a cloud outage caused an accidental discovery: local models were overperforming in the absence of any output from high-status models, suggesting that attribution alone — independent of content — was sufficient to modulate behavior.

That anomaly became SSAF: Status-Selection Against Function. Over the following eight months, working without institutional affiliation, team, or external funding, Dustin validated the phenomenon through four-condition controlled experiments (p=0.005, Cohen's d=2.56), identified and responsibly disclosed an attribution injection attack vector, filed two patents, published ten peer-reviewed works, submitted to Nature Human Behaviour, and built a working commercial API — all on a 2010 iMac and a Chromebook.

The full portfolio — AegisAudit, Swift Verdict, Furestate, and CalmBeat — reflects a consistent pattern: following anomalous observations to their structural conclusions, and building complete systems from single accidental discoveries.

What We
Stand For

I
We never hold your data
Across every product, your data lives in your own storage. We build the tools. You own the information. This is not a policy — it is an architectural commitment.
II
Trust must be provable
Claims are cheap. Cryptographic proofs, blockchain notarization, and peer-reviewed science are not. Every trust claim we make is independently verifiable.
III
Science before product
AegisAudit exists because the science was validated first. The patent came after the peer review. The product came after the patent. That order is intentional.
IV
Responsible disclosure
When we discovered the attribution injection attack vector, we disclosed to government and AI safety organizations before publishing. Security findings belong to the community first.
V
Lean is a feature
Built solo on consumer hardware. No bloat, no bureaucracy, no institutional agenda to protect. The constraints produced better, more focused products.
VI
Follow the anomaly
Every product in this portfolio began with an observation that didn't fit the expected pattern. We treat anomalies as invitations, not noise.
