CalmBeat · Android · Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026  ·  Pay Dirt Global LLC  ·  Northport, Alabama, United States
The Short Version
CalmBeat collects nothing.
No personal data. No analytics. No identifiers. No network calls. CalmBeat runs entirely on your device and never transmits any information to us or any third party.
1
Data We Collect

None. CalmBeat does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, location data, or any other information from you or your device.

2
Permissions

CalmBeat requests only the permissions required to function as a local wellness app (such as audio playback). No permission is used to access, collect, or transmit personal data.

3
Third-Party Services

CalmBeat does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or tracking libraries. There are no external data flows of any kind.

4
Children's Privacy

Because CalmBeat collects no data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 — and by design, we collect no information from anyone.

5
Health Data

CalmBeat is categorized as a health and wellness app. This section explicitly addresses health data as required by Google Play.

Health data collected: None. CalmBeat does not access, collect, read, process, transmit, or store any health data of any kind. This includes but is not limited to: heart rate, blood pressure, breathing rate, sleep data, fitness activity, body measurements, mental health information, medical records, or any other health or biometric information.

The app does not request permission to access any health-related APIs, sensors, or data sources on your device. No health data enters or leaves the app at any point during normal operation.

6
Data Retention

Retention period: None. Because CalmBeat does not collect any user data, there is no user data to retain. No personal information, usage data, health data, or any other user data is stored by Pay Dirt Global LLC — not on our servers, not in any database, and not in any third-party service.

The only data that exists on your device is the app itself and its built-in content, which is installed and managed entirely by you through the Google Play Store.

7
Data Deletion

Deletion request: Not applicable. Because CalmBeat collects no user data, there is nothing held by Pay Dirt Global LLC to delete on your behalf.

To remove all CalmBeat data from your device, simply uninstall the app through your device settings or the Google Play Store. Uninstalling removes all app data from your device.

If you believe we may hold any data about you in error, contact us at dustinjames@nextaitrust.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8
Medical Disclaimer

CalmBeat is a wellness and preparedness reference tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. In any medical emergency, call 911 immediately. CalmBeat is a supplement to — not a substitute for — emergency services.

9
Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, this page will be updated with a new effective date. Any future version of CalmBeat that collects data will require explicit user acknowledgment before doing so.
